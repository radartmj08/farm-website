About Us

At Farm Phoomjai and Jiaranai Garden, every harvest tells a story. We blend traditional farming wisdom with sustainable practices to grow produce that is both wholesome and unforgettable.

From locally nurtured soil to hand-picked vegetables and flowers, quality is at the heart of everything we do. Whether you're shopping for your weekly groceries or a special bouquet, we aim to bring freshness to every moment.

Our team is passionate about sustainability, flavor, and creating experiences that connect you with the land. Come visit — leave inspired.
